IMPROVE EMOTIONAL SPEECH SYNTHESIS BY USING SU CONSTRAINT VAE AND TEXT ENCODER AGGREGATION

Abstract: Emotion embedding learned from references is a straightforward approach for emotion speech synthesis in encoder-decoder systems. But how to get the embedding and how to inject the emotion embedding into TTS acoustic model are still under investigation. In this paper, we propose to use Spherical Mean with Uniform Variance (SU) constraint VAE to obtain a more focused emotion embedding. For the injection of emotion embedding, we introduce the information from emotion embedding to the multi-query attention in the text encoder aggregation, to learn a comprehensive sentence representation generating more expressive emotional speech. Experiments shows that the two strategies can produce more expressive emotional speech.
